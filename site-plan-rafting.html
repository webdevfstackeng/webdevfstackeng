Overview

Purpose

The purpose of Blue Water Rafting is not only to promote fun and healthy outdoor recreational activities. At Blue Water Rafting Company we also do rafting for business. We host companies on retreats and other groups of people for leisure and touring. Our website will searve as a powerful marketing tool for our business.

Audience

Our target audience are families on outdoor outings, tourists, schools and other businesses. We also offer rafting training programs. Our needs and desires are to grow the business to cater for larger group and to be able to host big competitions and tournaments. The public and potential customers will access our website through the link that will be shared with the public once we are satisfied with its design and development.
